Welcome to Japanese Language Tutoring System!

Study overview

We are researching methods for second language learning. When using this tool, we will collect anonymous data to improve the tool and understand second language learning better. You will see around 50 Japanese sentences and be asked how well you understand them. Afterwards, you will be given an estimation of how well you can understand articles in NHK Easy, a Japanese news website for language learners. You will also be asked to fill out a survey.

The system may take a few seconds to initialize, and you will see the nhk easy homepage during that time. We appreciate your patience.

Risks and benefits

There are no risks in this study beyond those in encountered in daily life. Your participation will help us improve our language learning tools. You will not be compensated for participation in this study.

Your answers will be confidential

The records of this study will be kept private. In any sort of report we make public we will not include any information that will make it possible to identify you. Research records will be kept encrypted on a lab machine; only the researchers will have access to the records.

Taking part is voluntary

Taking part in this study is completely voluntary. You may skip any part. If you decide not to take part or to skip some of the questions, it will not affect your current or future relationship with Cornell University. If you decide to take part, you are free to withdraw at any time.
